Research

I employ multi-method research.

My early graduate career work focused on Latinx political mobilization and vote choice culminating in two peer-reviewed co-authored articles. My articles in progress synthesize the empirical findings of my dissertation.

I specialize in the following subfields and topic areas:

  • American Politics—Political Behavior, Immigration and Criminal Justice Policy, and Intergroup Relations
  • Comparative Politics—The State and Identity Formation, Conflict and Revolution, and Political and Criminal Violence
